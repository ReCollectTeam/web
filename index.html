Additional Features

Your experience is important to us. In addition to its core functionality, ReCollect is equipped with a number of quality of life features that improve the saving experience.

Collaborative Collections

Collaborate with friends on shared collections to save trip ideas, memes, or any other content together in one place.

Light & Dark Mode

Our customizable UI allows you to switch freely between light and dark mode to suit your style and preferences.

Cross-Collection Saving

Find something on ReCollect you like? Add any public content directly to your collections with a single tap!

Source Sharing

All content on ReCollect is automatically linked to its original source, giving creators the credit that they deserve.

Intuitive Comment System

Content comments are compiled into one collection comment section, linking back to the media each of them came from.

Searchable Hashtags

Find public collections on the discover tab with user generated hashtags or author usernames.
